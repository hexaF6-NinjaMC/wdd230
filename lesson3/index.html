Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of the greatplainschamber.com website is to show businesses' issues and developments within the Great Plains area of the United States. This will be fulfilled by discussing how current events, like weather, disease/hardships, etc., have impacted businesses and their respective communities. This will then be summarized and presented, with pictures, on the main page.

A secondary agenda for the site is to set up membership opportunities, to further expand our influence and goal to provide, develop, and serve multiple communities within the Great Plains area. This could also be done through surrounding Chambers of Commerce in other geographies.
