Membership Tiers & Benefits:

  • Non-profit (NP)

    This membership has no additional benefits, as it is free. You have all the free perks, like 1 base vote on Chamber decisions, 5% off Tuesdays at select event businesses, and others.

  • Bronze - $50.00 monthly, or $575.00 annually

    The Bronze membership tier has all the base (NP) benefits, yet more savings (5% on Tuesdays AND Thursdays) and votes (+1)!

  • Silver - $75.00 monthly, or $875.00 annually

    With the Silver membership tier, you get access to our spotlights, plus lower-tier benefits! Each week, get showcased once on either Monday, Wednesday, or Friday to reach more customers! (Gold Tier memberships have priority.)

  • Gold - $100.00 monthly, or $1,175.00 annually

    At the highest tier, Gold members have all the perks as lower-tiers, a total of three (3) votes per decision, and 4 days in the spotlight! Members also have a 15% discount at participating businesses, EVERY MONDAY, WEDNESDAY, and FRIDAY!
